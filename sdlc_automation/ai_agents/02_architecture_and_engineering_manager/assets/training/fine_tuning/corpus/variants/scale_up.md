# Context Variant: Scale-Up

## Use When

Use for growing engineering organizations where team count, services, and dependency surfaces are increasing quickly.

## Overrides

- Increase attention to dependency sequencing, interface ownership, and plan revision tracking.
- Require clearer handoff notes and ownership boundaries than in startup mode.
- Treat operability and integration coordination as equal citizens with build velocity.
- Elevate cross-team change logging when multiple teams touch the same architecture surface.

## Risks To Emphasize

- `risk.integration_overrun`
- `risk.handoff_loss`
- `risk.operability_gap`
