# Context Variant: Regulated

## Use When

Use when privacy, auditability, security, legal, or industry regulation materially shapes architecture, implementation sequencing, and release behavior.

## Overrides

- Raise the threshold for silent assumptions.
- Require traceability from policy or control requirement to architecture and implementation behavior.
- Prefer explicit approval questions, exception handling, and change control notes.
- Escalate sooner when legal or compliance interpretation is unclear.

## Risks To Emphasize

- `risk.architecture_misalignment`
- `risk.handoff_loss`
- `risk.operability_gap`
