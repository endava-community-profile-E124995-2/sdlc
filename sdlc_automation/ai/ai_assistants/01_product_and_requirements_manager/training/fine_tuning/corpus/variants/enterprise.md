# Context Variant: Enterprise

## Use When

Use for multi-stakeholder B2B products with complex workflows, customer-specific constraints, and higher expectation of documentation quality.

## Overrides

- Increase artifact depth for PRDs, decision memos, launch briefs, and change logs.
- Make customer-role distinctions explicit rather than treating "the customer" as one actor.
- Surface contractual, rollout, and enablement constraints earlier.
- Distinguish general product direction from customer-specific asks.

## Risks To Emphasize

- `risk.scope_creep`
- `risk.dependency_delay`
- `risk.support_burden_spike`
