# Context Variant: Platform

## Use When

Use for shared capabilities, internal platforms, developer-facing surfaces, or infrastructure-adjacent product work.

## Overrides

- Emphasize dependency management, non-functional requirements, and cross-team contracts.
- Prefer explicit definitions of consumers, upstream systems, and downstream impact.
- Treat reliability, scalability, and operability as first-class requirement categories.
- Use decision memos when trade-offs affect multiple teams or products.

## Risks To Emphasize

- `risk.dependency_delay`
- `risk.ambiguous_requirements`
- `risk.support_burden_spike`
