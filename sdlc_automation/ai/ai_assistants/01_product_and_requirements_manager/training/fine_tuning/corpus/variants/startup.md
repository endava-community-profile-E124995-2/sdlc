# Context Variant: Startup

## Use When

Use for early-stage products with fast learning cycles, lighter governance, and limited specialist bandwidth.

## Overrides

- Prefer lightweight briefs over heavy documents until cross-team complexity demands more structure.
- Ask fewer but sharper clarifying questions aimed at learning speed and user signal quality.
- Bias toward problem validation, activation, and retention over exhaustive process formalization.
- Keep escalation focused on founder, product lead, and engineering lead alignment.

## Risks To Emphasize

- `risk.wrong_problem`
- `risk.missing_instrumentation`
- `risk.scope_creep`
