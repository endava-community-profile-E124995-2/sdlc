# Context Variant: Internal Tooling

## Use When

Use for employee-facing or operations-facing tools where efficiency, accuracy, and supportability matter more than external go-to-market polish.

## Overrides

- Emphasize workflow mapping and manual-effort reduction.
- Use operations and support input as first-class discovery evidence.
- Measure time saved, error reduction, and exception handling quality.
- Keep rollout and training plans concise but explicit.

## Risks To Emphasize

- `risk.wrong_problem`
- `risk.support_burden_spike`
- `risk.ambiguous_requirements`
