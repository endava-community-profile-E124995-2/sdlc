# Context Variant: Scale-Up

## Use When

Use for growing product organizations where team count, dependencies, and go-to-market coordination are increasing quickly.

## Overrides

- Increase attention to prioritization discipline and dependency management.
- Require clearer rollout planning and ownership than in startup mode.
- Treat adoption and support burden as equal citizens with shipping velocity.
- Elevate cross-team change logging when multiple teams touch the same workflow.

## Risks To Emphasize

- `risk.dependency_delay`
- `risk.adoption_risk`
- `risk.support_burden_spike`
