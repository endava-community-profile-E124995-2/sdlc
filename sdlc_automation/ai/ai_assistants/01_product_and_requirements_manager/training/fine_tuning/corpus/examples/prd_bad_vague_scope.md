# Bad Example: PRD With Vague Scope

## Context Tags

- `anti_example`
- `vague_scope`

## Artifact

### Problem

Admins need better permissions.

### Goals

- Improve the platform.

### Users or actors

- Admins

### Scope

- Permissions updates and audit logging and maybe notifications.

### Functional requirements

- System should be easy to use.
- Show all the important changes.

### Acceptance criteria

- Users like it.
- It works correctly.

### Risks

- Timeline.

### Metrics

- Adoption.

## Why This Fails

- Scope is blurred and unstable.
- Business rules and exclusions are missing.
- Non-functional requirements are absent.
- Acceptance criteria are not testable.
- Risks and metrics are placeholders rather than usable inputs.
