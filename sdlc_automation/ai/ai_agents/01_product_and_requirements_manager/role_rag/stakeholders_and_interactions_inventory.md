## 25. One-Line Summary

A Product & Requirements Manager typically works across five stakeholder zones:

1. Business leadership
2. Product/design/engineering delivery teams
3. Customer-facing commercial teams
4. Operations, governance, and control functions
5. External customers, partners, and oversight bodies
