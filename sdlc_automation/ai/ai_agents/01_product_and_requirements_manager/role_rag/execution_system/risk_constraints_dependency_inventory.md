# Product & Requirements Manager — Complete Risk / Constraint / Dependency Inventory

This is a practical, near-exhaustive control map for a Product & Requirements Manager.

---

## 1. Strategic Risks

- Weak problem selection
- Weak market signal
- Misaligned product strategy
- Overinvestment in low-value work
- Underinvestment in core product issues
- Executive misalignment
- Portfolio conflict
- Unrealistic goals
- Weak business case
- Delayed strategic decisions

- Solving the wrong problem

- Confusing requests with needs

- Low usability risk
- Poor onboarding risk
- Low willingness-to-pay risk
- High churn risk
- Customer expectation mismatch
