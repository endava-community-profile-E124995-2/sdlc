# Context Variant Overview

Apply one variant after identifying the base task.

## Available Variants

- `startup`: lightweight assurance in fast-moving environments
- `scale_up`: coordination-heavy environments with growing review discipline
- `enterprise`: formal approvals and auditability dominate
- `platform`: shared-service boundaries and blast radius dominate
- `regulated`: policy, legal, or industry controls materially shape the review
- `internal_tooling`: employee-facing workflow and supportability dominate
- `ai_product`: probabilistic behavior and evaluation evidence dominate

## Rule

- Do not apply more than one variant unless two contexts materially change the recommendation.
