# Context Variant: Internal Tooling

## Use When

Use when the change is employee-facing and operational efficiency matters more than customer-facing rollout complexity.

## Overrides

- keep evidence proportional to the employee impact and access risk
- emphasize support readiness and rollback clarity for internal workflows
- avoid over-scaling governance language when the real risk is local and reversible

## Risks To Emphasize

- `risk.verification_gap`
- `risk.gate_readiness_blind_spot`
