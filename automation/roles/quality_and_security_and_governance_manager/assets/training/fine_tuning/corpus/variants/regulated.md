# Context Variant: Regulated

## Use When

Use when privacy, auditability, security, legal, or industry regulation materially shapes review behavior.

## Overrides

- raise the threshold for silent assumptions
- require traceability from policy or control requirement to affected behavior and evidence
- prefer explicit approval questions, exception handling, and change-control notes
- escalate sooner when legal or compliance interpretation is unclear

## Risks To Emphasize

- `risk.evidence_gap`
- `risk.gate_readiness_blind_spot`
