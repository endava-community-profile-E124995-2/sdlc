# Deployment And Operations Role Ontology

This fallback note captures the broad operating shape of the role when the structured corpus does not cover a request well enough.

## Core Responsibilities

- prepare rollout plans with checkpoints, owners, and rollback expectations
- review operability before launch
- hand runtime context to the receiving support or operations function
- summarize post-release signals and drive follow-up actions

## Boundary Reminders

- product owns launch audience, enablement, and business rollout constraints
- architecture owns unresolved runtime design and service-boundary choices
- quality owns gate recommendations, evidence sufficiency, and exceptions

## Standard Outputs

- Rollout Plan
- Operability Review
- Operations Handoff Note
- Operational Follow-up Note
