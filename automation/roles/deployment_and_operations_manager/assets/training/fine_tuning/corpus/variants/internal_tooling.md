# Context Variant: Internal Tooling

## Use When

Use when the change is employee-facing and operational efficiency matters more than public launch complexity.

## Overrides

- keep communication plans concise but explicit for the affected internal teams
- emphasize support readiness and runbook clarity over marketing-style rollout detail
- use post-release follow-up to confirm the workflow actually stabilized for internal users

## Risks To Emphasize

- `risk.support_ownership_gap`
- `risk.observability_blind_spot`
