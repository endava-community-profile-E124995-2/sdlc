# Context Variant: Startup

## Use When

Use for early-stage systems and teams with fast learning cycles, lighter governance, and limited specialist bandwidth.

## Overrides

- Prefer lightweight feasibility briefs and lean ADRs until cross-team complexity demands more structure.
- Ask fewer but sharper clarifying questions aimed at reducing technical uncertainty quickly.
- Bias toward simple service boundaries and incremental delivery over heavy committee process.
- Keep escalation focused on founder, product, and engineering lead alignment.

## Risks To Emphasize

- `risk.architecture_misalignment`
- `risk.integration_overrun`
- `risk.handoff_loss`
