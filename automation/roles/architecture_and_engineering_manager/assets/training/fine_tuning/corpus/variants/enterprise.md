# Context Variant: Enterprise

## Use When

Use for multi-team environments with formal reviews, shared platforms, customer-specific constraints, and higher expectations for technical documentation quality.

## Overrides

- Increase artifact depth for feasibility briefs, ADRs, execution plans, and handoff notes.
- Make service-owner and review-owner distinctions explicit rather than treating engineering as one actor.
- Surface contractual, control, and runtime constraints earlier.
- Distinguish general platform direction from account-specific exceptions.

## Risks To Emphasize

- `risk.architecture_misalignment`
- `risk.integration_overrun`
- `risk.operability_gap`
