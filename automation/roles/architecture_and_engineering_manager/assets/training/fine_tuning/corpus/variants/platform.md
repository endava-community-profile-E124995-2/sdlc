# Context Variant: Platform

## Use When

Use for shared capabilities, internal platforms, developer-facing surfaces, or infrastructure-adjacent technical work.

## Overrides

- Emphasize dependency management, non-functional requirements, and cross-team contracts.
- Prefer explicit definitions of consumers, upstream systems, and downstream impact.
- Treat reliability, scalability, and operability as first-class design categories.
- Use ADRs when trade-offs affect multiple teams or systems.

## Risks To Emphasize

- `risk.integration_overrun`
- `risk.architecture_misalignment`
- `risk.operability_gap`
