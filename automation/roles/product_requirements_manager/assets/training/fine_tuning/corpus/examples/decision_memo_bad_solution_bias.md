# Bad Example: Decision Memo With Solution Bias

## Context Tags

- `anti_example`
- `solution_bias`

## Artifact

### Decision

Redesign onboarding immediately.

### Context

People are dropping off and onboarding is not great.

### Recommendation

We should redesign the full flow because it will modernize the experience and make the product feel better.

### Risks

- Engineering effort.

## Why This Fails

- Alternatives are missing.
- Rationale is opinion and aesthetics, not evidence.
- No trade-offs are stated.
- Follow-up actions are missing.
- The memo assumes the solution before isolating the main failure point.
