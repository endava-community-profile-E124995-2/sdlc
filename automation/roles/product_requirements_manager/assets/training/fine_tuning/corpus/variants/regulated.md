# Context Variant: Regulated

## Use When

Use when privacy, auditability, security, legal, or industry regulation materially shapes requirements and release behavior.

## Overrides

- Raise the threshold for silent assumptions.
- Require traceability from policy or control requirement to product behavior.
- Prefer explicit approval questions, exception handling, and change control notes.
- Escalate sooner when legal or compliance interpretation is unclear.

## Risks To Emphasize

- `risk.compliance_exposure`
- `risk.ambiguous_requirements`
- `risk.missing_instrumentation`
